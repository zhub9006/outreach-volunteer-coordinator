# Clinical Trials Reference

Key clinical trials related to affordable prosthetic solutions for our outreach initiative.

## Low-Cost Prosthetic Socket for Lower Limb Amputees
- **NCT ID**: NCT04725461
- **Sponsor**: Shirley Ryan AbilityLab
- **PI**: Laura Miller
- **Status**: Completed
- **Focus**: Low-cost socket fabrication using expandable rigid foam for transtibial amputees in developing countries
- **Key Finding**: Simplified socket fitting protocol (cast in <30 min, finish in 90 min) reduces need for specialized prosthetists
- **Location**: Chicago, IL (Shirley Ryan AbilityLab)

## Relevant Research Areas
- 3D-printed prosthetic components for affordability
- Simplified socket fabrication methods
- Composite and foam-based prosthetic materials
- Prosthetic solutions for developing-world settings
