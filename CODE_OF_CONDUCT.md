# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and leaders pledge to make participation in our prosthetic outreach initiative a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Share constructive feedback
- Prioritize the mission over individual recognition
- Welcome newcomers and support learning

## Enforcement
Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Report concerns to the core team.
